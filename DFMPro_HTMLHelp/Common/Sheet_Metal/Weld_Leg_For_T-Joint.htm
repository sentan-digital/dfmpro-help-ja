Weld Leg for T- Joint

Rule

The fillet weld size should be appropriate to provide sufficient joint strength and to avoid breakage due to over weld and under weld.

 

Explanation

Fillet weld sizes should be equal. The equal weld leg requires less time, less weld metal, and less heat input. It also improves the weld joint. This rule supports T-joint, where the angle between the plates to join is 90 degrees.
